## About

**ReQuest** is the result of the final project of _[Diogo Carvalho](https://github.com/carvalho28)_, a Bachelor's degree student in _Computer Science and Engineering_.

As the sole developer and creator of ReQuest, I invested considerable time and effort into designing and implementing this innovative web application. The project represents the culmination of his academic journey, showcasing his skills, knowledge, and passion for software development, requirement management, and **creating software to solve users' problems**.

I hope the application and documentation serve as a valuable resource for students (as I am also one). By using it, you can streamline your requirement management workflow, enhance collaboration, and drive the success of your software development projects.

Thank you for choosing **ReQuest**!
